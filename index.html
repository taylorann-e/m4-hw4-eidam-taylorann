Cody Johnson

About Cody Johnson

Cody Johnson is an american country music artist. He is known for bringing back "real" country music. He has electric concerts and songs that continually top the music charts. He has received multiple industry honors and 7.5 billion streams. If you want to know even more about him as an artist you can click here to be taken to his brand webpage.

Discography

Cody Johnson released his first album Six Strings One Dream in 2009 and his latest album Leather was released in 2023. His number one song, Dirt Cheap, is followed closely by 'Til You Can't, and On My Way to You. He has also put out a live recording album as well.

Why Cody Johnson Is My Favorite

Cody Johnson, also known as CoJo, is my favorite artist because of his authenticity. He writes what is on his heart whether it is relevant in pop culture or not. He speaks and mind and it is reflected in his lyrics and music. He adores his wife, and respects a traditional lifestyle. He reminds me of the country artists I grew up listening to.
